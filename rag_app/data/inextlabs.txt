iNextLabs Knowledge Base
iNextLabs is a Cloud and AI-focused company that designs intelligent AI agents built on the inFlow platform. The platform consists of three main products: inFlow DocsAI (automates document-heavy workflows by extracting, summarizing, and classifying data from various file types), inFlow InsightsAI (delivers advanced analytics to predict trends and provide actionable recommendations), and inFlow EngageAI (deploys multilingual AI agents for automated support and personalized interactions across WhatsApp, web, and voice channels). These solutions serve multiple industries including Insurance & Consumer, Finance & Procurement, B2B Sales & Marketing, and Banking.
The company emphasizes enterprise-grade security, data privacy, and ethical AI governance while offering a connected ecosystem that integrates workflows and applications across organizations. iNextLabs partners with major technology companies including Microsoft, Google, Meta, and Salesforce. They operate from Singapore with additional presence in India, Malaysia, and USA, and offer an AI Discovery Workshop to help businesses validate use cases, quantify ROI, and build implementation roadmaps.
